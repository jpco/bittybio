bitty.bio summaries of you & your things

bitty.bio is a business-card-sized social network: an itty-bitty service to create small & simple bios, business cards, home pages, or what have you. it is designed to be simple, small, and succinct. It can also (hopefully) connect to the more “full-featured” social networks for those who want more complete functionality.
The only one that exists is bb@jpco, so... there y'go.
